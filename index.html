The Problem with Pop-ups

In the world of e-commerce, businesses often rely on traditional pop-up modals to capture a user's attention. However, these aggressive interruptions often backfire, leading to a negative customer experience.

  • They are an aggressive and frustrating form of interruption.
  • They cause friction and often fail to deliver on their goal.
  • They lead to lost conversion opportunities and a high bounce rate.

My Solution: User-Triggered Panels

We've developed a better alternative: optimized slide-in panels. This approach changes the dynamic completely from forced interaction to user choice.

  • Panels are triggered by subtle, sticky buttons that live on the same page.
  • The user chooses to engage, demonstrating genuine interest.
  • This delivers a more respectful and effective customer journey.

Value on the Same Page
